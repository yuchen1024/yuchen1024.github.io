Brief Introduction

My research interest is provably secure cryptography, with particular focus on fundemental primitives and their applications on high-level protocols.

From August 2015, I am an visiting scholar of crypto group at CUHK, working with my host Sherman S. M. Chow

  • 09/2009 - 09/2010: School of Computing, Dublin City University, CSC Joint Ph.D. Program (Advisor Michael Scott)
  • 09/2002 - 07/2006: School of Computer Science and Engineering, Hefei University of Technology, Bachelor in Information Security.
